Recipe

Chicken and Spinach Yoghurt Rice

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast - 2 pieces
  • White Rice - 1 cup
  • Spinach - 2 cups
  • Plain Yoghurt - 1 cup
  • Bread - 2 slices (Optional)
  • Asparagus - 5 stems
  • Kale - 1 cup (Optional)
  • Salt and Pepper - to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rice: Rinse 1 cup of rice under cold water until water runs clear. Boil rice in a 3:1 water-to-rice ratio until it's cooked and fluffy. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the chicken: Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill chicken until fully cooked and juices run clear. Then slice into thin strips.
  3. Sauté vegetables: On medium heat, saute asparagus and spinach until tender. If using kale, add it last as it cooks quickly.
  4. Mix ingredients in a bowl: In a large bowl, mix the cooked rice, chicken strips and sautéed vegetables. Top with a cup of plain yoghurt and gently stir until everything is well combined.
  5. Serve: You can serve this dish with slices of bread on the side if you prefer. Enjoy your homemade Chicken and Spinach Yoghurt Rice!
